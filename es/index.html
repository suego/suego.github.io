03. Programs

Innovation is everywhere

SUEGO has been an implementing partner for various startup programs across world-renown organizations. And recently has started to bake in additional improvements to increase the breadth and depth of its impact and outcomes.

01. Techstars Startup Weekend

Techstars Startup Weekend is an action-packed three day program, where aspiring entrepreneurs get to experience the highs, lows, fun and pressure that make up life at a startup. All in just 54 hours.

02. Kauffman Foundation FastTrac

Kauffman FastTrac equips aspiring entrepreneurs with the business skills and insights, tools, resources, and peer networks necessary to start and grow successful businesses.

03. Techstars Startup Week

Free and open to everyone, Startup Week is a celebration designed to support and connect entrepreneurs, aspiring founders, innovation leaders, investors, technologists, innovation leaders, and those interested in the startup community.

04. FoundersBoost Pre-Accelerator

A global tech startup pre-accelerator aimed at working with great early stage entrepreneurs to prepare them for accelerator programs, seed investment, and revenue through a six week part-time program.

05. OpenExO ExO Sprints

The ExO Sprint is a ten-week process of consulting organizations on how to apply the principles of exponential organizations to generate accelerated growth. The goal is to take an organization two years ahead in only ten weeks.

06. Global Entrepreneurship Network Ecosystem Development

A strong entrepreneurial ecosystem is key to job growth and economic development, but it can be challenging to build. We partner with city stakeholders to help local companies experience organic company creation.
